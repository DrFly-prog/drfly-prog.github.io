Privacy Policy
Tangram Infinity
Last updated: March 6, 2026

Overview

Tangram Infinity is a puzzle game developed by an independent creator. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data.

Data We Collect

The App itself does not collect any personal information. However, we use third-party services that may collect data:

  • Google AdMob — used to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address, and usage data to serve relevant ads.
  • Game progress — your score and game state are stored locally on your device only using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device.

Advertising

The App displays ads through Google AdMob, including interstitial and rewarded video ads. We request non-personalized ads where possible. For more information: policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties, except as required by Google AdMob as described above.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

Data Retention

Local game data is stored on your device and can be cleared at any time by uninstalling the App. We do not retain any data on our own servers.

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, delete or opt out of personalized advertising. On Android: Settings → Google → Ads. On iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
