// ABOUT THE GAME

What it is.

A spatial packing puzzle that quietly turns into an obsession.

You have a container. You have items. Your only mission: pack everything in, optimally. The better the fit, the higher your Space Score. The higher your score, the harder it gets to stop.

Like Tetris, but slower. Like Lego, but less painful. A satisfying spatial loop with seasonal levels and a Daily Pack challenge.

// LEGAL · PRIVACY POLICY

Privacy policy.

Last updated: April 23, 2026

Overview

PackIt Infinity is a puzzle game developed by DrFly Studio, an independent developer. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data is collected, how it is used, and your rights regarding that data.

Data we collect

The App itself does not collect any personal information. However, we use third-party services that may collect data:

  • Google AdMob — used to display advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address, and usage data to serve relevant ads.
  • Firebase Analytics — used to understand how players interact with the app (session length, level completion rates, Space Score averages). No personally identifiable information is collected.
  • Firebase Firestore — used to deliver the Daily Pack (one shared packing challenge per day). Only an anonymous session identifier and your Daily Pack result are stored.
  • Game progress — your score and game state are stored locally on your device only using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device.

Advertising

The App displays ads through Google AdMob, including interstitial ads (between levels, at most once every three levels, never during a puzzle) and rewarded video ads (only when you choose to watch one for an optimal hint or extra moves). We request non-personalized ads where possible. For more information: policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

Data sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties, except as required by Google AdMob and Firebase as described above.

Children's privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal information, please contact us so we can delete it.

Data retention

Local game data is stored on your device and can be cleared at any time by uninstalling the App. Anonymous Daily Pack results stored on Firebase are automatically purged after 90 days. We do not retain any personal data on our own servers.

Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, delete or opt out of personalized advertising. On Android: Settings → Google → Ads. On iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking.

Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
